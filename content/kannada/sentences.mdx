# Kannada Sentences :icon[MessageSquareText]

Kannada often places the verb at the end of the sentence.

## Examples

| Kannada | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| ನಾನು ಓದುತ್ತೇನೆ. | I read. |
| ಅವಳು ಬರೆಯುತ್ತಾಳೆ. | She writes. |
| ಅವರು ಬರುತ್ತಾರೆ. | They come. |

## Reveal Practice

In `ನಾನು ಓದುತ್ತೇನೆ`, the subject is [[ನಾನು]].

:::warning
Spoken Kannada can differ by region. Keep listening to real usage as you learn written forms.
:::
