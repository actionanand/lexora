# Kannada Words :icon[Sparkles]

Group new words by daily use. That makes recall much easier.

## Useful Words

| Kannada | Transliteration | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- |
| ನೀರು | neeru | water |
| ಮನೆ | mane | house |
| ಪುಸ್ತಕ | pustaka | book |
| ಸ್ನೇಹಿತ | snehita | friend |

:::info
Kannada has formal and informal choices in conversation. Learn a phrase with its social context when possible.
:::

The word for house is [[ಮನೆ]].
