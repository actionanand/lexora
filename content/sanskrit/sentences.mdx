# Sanskrit Sentences :icon[MessageSquareText]

Sentences become easier when you track the verb and then ask who or what is involved.

## Examples

| Sanskrit | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| बालकः पठति। | The boy reads. |
| सीता लिखति। | Sita writes. |
| मित्रं आगच्छति। | The friend comes. |

## Reveal Practice

In `बालकः पठति`, the verb is [[पठति]].

:::note
Many beginner sentences place the subject first and verb last, but Sanskrit can move words around while keeping meaning through endings.
:::
