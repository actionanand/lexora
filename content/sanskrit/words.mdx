# Sanskrit Words :icon[Sparkles]

Build vocabulary in small clusters. A cluster helps each word carry context.

## Everyday Words

| Word | Transliteration | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- |
| जलम् | jalam | water |
| पुस्तकम् | pustakam | book |
| गुरु | guru | teacher |
| मित्रम् | mitram | friend |

## Practice

The word for water is [[जलम्]].

:::tip
Write each word once, say it twice, and use it in one sentence.
